About

Hi! I'm Vedansh!


I am a rising senior at Archbishop Mitty High School and planning on dual-majoring in Mechanical Engineering and Business.

I'm passionate about rapid fabrication and it's applications. My fascination for additive manufacturing began shortly after Maker Faire where I was first introduced to 3D printers. Over the last 6-7 years, I have nurtured this inital curiosty of mine into my passion.

Right now, I am working at Lockheed Martin Space as an Engineer Intern at ADMC (Additive Design Manufacturing Center). While I am unable to disclose specific projects that I am working on, all of my projects utilize the skills that I have taught myself: CAD/CAM, FEA (Finite Element Analysis), Material Properties Simulation/Optimization, Machining, and, most importantly, collaboration.

When I am not working, you'll find me biking and playing football with my friends, fixing/improving things around the house, or trying to teach myself a new skill—recently, web design.
